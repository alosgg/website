What is DDoS Protection?

+
DDoS Protection is a security service that safeguards your online infrastructure against distributed denial-of-service attacks, ensuring your services remain available to legitimate users.

How fast can ALOS respond to attacks?

+
Our automated systems detect and respond to attacks within seconds, with our global network of PoPs ensuring minimal latency and maximum protection.

Do you offer custom solutions?

+
Yes, we offer customized enterprise solutions tailored to your specific needs, infrastructure, and security requirements.

Is your WAF (Web application firewall) limited?

What protection does ALOS use?

+
ALOS employs a robust, three-layer firewall structure consisting of XDP, iptables, and application-level filtering. This setup enables significant traffic reduction, filtering incoming requests from 1 million REQ/s down to just 15k REQ/s, providing high-performance security and efficiency.
